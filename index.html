y/doods

Everybody doodles. Whether it was 15 years ago in your high school math class about that dreamy boy who was angstily struggling with y=mx+b or 10 minutes ago in your 2 hour meeting about OKRs, we all know the familiar feeling of scratching meaningless hearts or lines across our notebooks. It is a kind of subconscious expression of our less important thoughts, and I think it says a lot about us as people. Therefore, I wanted to use these little doodles to document all the amazing and diverse people we have at yelp! I went around asking everyone here from Creative, to Sales, to Engineering for their doodle and got to know some really cool people because of it. So here - take a look at all the artistic, and maybe less artistic doodles our friends have to offer.
